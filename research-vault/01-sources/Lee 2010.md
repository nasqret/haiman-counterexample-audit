## Central assertions

- Theorem A: the principal component of \(\operatorname{Hilb}^9(\mathbb C^8)\) is not locally Cohen-Macaulay at \(\mathfrak m^2\).
- Theorem 3 / Proposition 4: the defining ideal \(J_8\) has a minimal degree-90 generator in a specified Schur module.
- Lemma 19: supplies the missing computational bridge, but only as a sketch.
