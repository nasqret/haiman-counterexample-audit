# Notation and objects

Let \(W\cong\mathbb C^d\). The symmetric affine chart \(V_d\subset\operatorname{Hilb}^{d+1}(\mathbb C^d)\) contains \(\mathfrak m^2\) and is described through ideal projectors
\[
P:\mathbb C[x_1,\ldots,x_d]\longrightarrow \operatorname{span}(1,x_1,\ldots,x_d).
\]

Coordinates are defined by
\[
P(x_i x_j)=p_{0,ij}+\sum_{m=1}^d p_{m,ij}x_m,\qquad p_{m,ij}=p_{m,ji}.
\]

For \(a\notin\{i,j,k\}\), the quadratic relation used in Lemma 19 is
\[
C(a;j,(i,k))=\sum_{m=1}^d
\bigl(p_{m,ij}p_{a,km}-p_{m,kj}p_{a,im}\bigr).
\]

After eliminating translations, the nontrivial coordinate representation is
\[
S_{(3,1,1,\ldots,1,0)}W,
\]
of dimension
\[
d\left(\binom{d+1}{2}-1\right).
\]
For \(d=8\), this is \(8(36-1)=280\).

The reduced principal component in this chart is
\[
P_d\cong\mathbb C^d\times\operatorname{Spec}(R_d/J_d).
\]
