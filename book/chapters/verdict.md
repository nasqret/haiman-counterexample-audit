# Verdict

The audit is in progress. No independently verified counterexample or impossibility result is claimed yet.

Two printed representation subclaims in Lemma 19 are now refuted as written:

- not every maximal minor belongs to the claimed irreducible Schur module;
- not every $89\times89$ minor belongs to one of the 15 listed predecessor modules.

A weight-compatible nonzero maximal minor also fails the highest-weight annihilation test. These are genuine gaps in the written proof, but they are not yet a disproof of the theorem because another linear combination or isotypic projection may still produce the required constituent.
:::

The final version of this chapter will state exactly one of:

1. **Verified counterexample:** all computational and geometric obligations close.
2. **Refuted construction:** a required identity or implication fails, with a reproducible counter-certificate.
3. **Conditional/unresolved:** the smallest remaining obligation is isolated, with all other edges certified.

Publication history, plausibility, and agreement of several black-box computations are not substitutes for this classification.
